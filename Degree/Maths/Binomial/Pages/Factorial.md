### The Factorial Function


The factorial function is defined as follows:

*For every positive integer $n$ in $\mathbb{N}$ we define*
$$n!=1\times2\times3\times...\times(n-2)\times(n-1)\times n$$

Also $0!=1$.
Just accept it.

Notice that $n!=n\times(n-1)!$
