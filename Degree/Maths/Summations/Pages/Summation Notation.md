### Summation Notation

It gets quite long to write out $$f(0)+f(1)+f(2)+f(3)+f(4)+f(5)+...+f(734)$$ so we have notation to simplify it.
$$\sum_{n=0}^{734}f\left(n\right)$$
The variable $n$ is a dummy variable here and can be anything.
