This technique uses [[Partial Fractions]] to split up quotients of polynomials.
### Example 1
$$
\begin{align*}
\int \frac{x^{3}}{x^{2} - 4} dx &= \int x + \frac{2}{x-2} + \frac{2}{x+2}\\
\\
&= \frac{x^{2}}{2} = 2 \ln|x-2| + 2 \ln|x+2| + c
\end{align*}
$$
