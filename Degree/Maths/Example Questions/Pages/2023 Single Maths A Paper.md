### Q1
##### Part i
![[IMAGE SMA 2023 Q1i.png]]

$$
\DeclareMathOperator{arcsinh}{arcsinh}
\begin{align*}
y &= \arcsinh x \\
\\
\sinh y &= x\\
\\
\cosh y \frac{dy}{dx} &= 1\\
\\
\frac{dy}{dx} &= \frac{1}{\cosh y}\\
\\
&\text{Using } \cosh^{2} x - \sinh^{2} x \equiv 1 \\
\\
\frac{dy}{dx} &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{1+\sinh^{2} y}}\\
\\
\frac{dy}{dx} &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{1+x^{2}}}
\end{align*}
$$
