### The Reciprocals

$$\begin{aligned}
\DeclareMathOperator{cosech}{cosech}
\DeclareMathOperator{sech}{sech}
\cosech{x}&=\frac{1}{\sinh x} \\\\
\sech x &= \frac{1}{\cosh x} \\\\
\coth x &= \frac{1}{\tanh x}
\end{aligned}$$
