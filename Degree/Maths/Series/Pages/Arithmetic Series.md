![[Series MOC 🌍]]

A series where $a_{n} = n$, so $S_{N} = \sum^{N}_{n=1}n$.

$$
\begin{align*}
S_{N} &= 1 + 2 + 3 + \dots + (N-1) + N\\
S_{N} &= N + (N-1) + (N-2) + \dots + 2 + 1\\
2S_{N} &= (N+1) + (N+1) + (N+1) + \dots + (N+1)\\
2 S_{N} &= N(N+1)\\
\\
S_{N} &= \frac{N(N+1)}{2}
\end{align*}
$$

$S_N$ has no limit as $N\rightarrow \infty$, so the series is divergent.
